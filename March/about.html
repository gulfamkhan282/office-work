About Us

Welcome to our Shop! We have been in this business since 2008. We are dealing in Colored Gemstones like Tourmaline, Aquamarine, Kunzite, Topaz, Sphene, Garnet, Quartz & Peridot etc. We mainly deal in Gemstones from Pakistan & Afghanistan origin.
Northern areas of Pakistan have very famous mines producing many unique and high quality of gemstones.
We have direct access to miners to facilitate our valued buyers offering them competitive quality prices to help them grow their businesses.
Our priority is to maintain good relation with our customers.
If you are looking for a perfect coloured gemstones, then you have come to the right place. We are well-established, professional gemstone dealers based in the Pakistan, and deal only with the highest quality gemstones.
Our cutting and polishing is to the highest standard along with symmetry,
we cut with beauty being the priority and not the finished weight! All stones are 100% natural with accurate descriptions, highly magnified photographic images so that every detail is visible, and all treatments are fully disclosed.
